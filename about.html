Davy Jones (Lead Vocals)

David Thomas Jones was born 30 December 1945 in Lancashire. Davy has been a member of the pop-rock group The Monkees since 1966 and is an established singer/song writer.

Micky Dolenz (Drums)

George Michael Dolenz was born on March 8, 1945 in Los Angeles. Micky is an American actor, musician, television director, radio personality and theater director, but he is best known as a vocalist and drummer of The Monkees. He has been the band's drummer since 1966 and is still going strong.

Michael Nesmith(Singer/Song writer)

Michael Nesmith, Born in Texas in 1942, Nesmith served in the U.S. Air Force before pursuing a career in music. He signed on to become a member of the Monkees in 1965

Peter Tork (Singer, Guitarist)

Peter Tork was born February 13, 1942 in Washington, D.C. Peter joined the Monkees in 1966 as their guitaist.
